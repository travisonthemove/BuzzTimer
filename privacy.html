Privacy

Your buzz, your data.

BuzzTimer is designed to keep things simple and local:

  • No accounts, no social features.

    You don’t need a login to use BuzzTimer. It’s built as a single-page web app.

  • Data stays on your device.

    Session logs, notes, and settings are stored in your browser (for example, using local storage). The app does not maintain a central database of your entries, and your data is not sold.

  • No targeted ads or hidden third-party trackers in the core app experience.

    BuzzTimer isn’t built around ad tech. If this ever changes, this page will be updated before any new tracking goes live.

  • What you write is for you.

    Your sessions, moments, and ideas are meant for your own reflection. If you share screenshots or export anything, remember you’re the one choosing where that information goes.

BuzzTimer is not a secure medical record system and shouldn’t be used as one. Avoid entering sensitive personal details that you wouldn’t be comfortable having on your device.

If a future version adds features like cloud backup, accounts, or optional analytics, this Privacy section will be updated in clear language so you can make an informed choice.
