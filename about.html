About BuzzTimer

BuzzTimer is a mindful companion for your cannabis sessions.

The goal isn’t to chase the biggest high. BuzzTimer is here for purposeful use: to help you notice what you’re taking, how it feels, and what you might want to repeat—or avoid—next time.

BuzzTimer focuses on three simple flows:

  1. Session Tracking – Log what you used, how much, and when. See your sessions as moments in time instead of a blur.
  2. Mindfulness – Capture check-ins and “High Ideas” so you don’t lose important thoughts, insights, or feelings.
  3. Entertainment – When you’re buzzing and need a distraction, BuzzTimer offers gentle, low-pressure options like the Entertainment Zone, where you can step away from overthinking without leaving the app ecosystem.

BuzzTimer does not tell you what to use, how much to take, or how to treat any condition. It’s a personal journaling and awareness tool for adults in places where cannabis is legal. Please follow your local laws, listen to your body, and treat this app as a helpful companion—not medical or mental health advice.

How BuzzTimer was created

BuzzTimer started as a high-vibe coding project in early 2024. Around that time, tools like Cursor and Windsurf appeared, claiming to offer a new level of AI coding assistance. In 2025, the OpenAI Codex CLI, a command-line tool, arrived. BuzzTimer felt like a fun, focused project to learn and test this new way of working with AI outside of cumbersome visual interfaces.

BuzzTimer V1 is the result of a High Idea combined with the release of a more refined coding assistant that could be used directly from the command line. If you’re curious, you can review the code and follow development at the BuzzTimer GitHub repository:

https://github.com/travisonthemove/BuzzTimer
